About Us

Our goal is to do produce 100% organic CBD using only the natural ecosystem that surrounds us as a fertilizer and utilizing modern day DIY robotics solutions to digitalize and understand the system of relations between plants in order to build machines that work with it for everyone.

The Tech

Our goal is the automation of all the stages of a plants life until harvest time. Everyone should be able to grow their own organic crops without missing out at work and in flexibility. DiY robotics and the Internet of Things makes it possible. We make it accessible. Our open-source approach invites everyone to design their own robots, based on teir needs.
